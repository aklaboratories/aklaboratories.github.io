02HALO

Your music,
wearing glass.

Any appAlbum-colour meshCompact mode

Halo floats a see-through widget of whatever's playing — Spotify, Apple Music, a browser tab, a podcast. The backdrop is a living mesh gradient pulled straight from the album art. Hit minimize and it shrinks to just the cover.

  • Universal now-playing
  • Mesh backdrop from album colours
  • Compact cover-only mode
  • Ambient mode — fades when idle, returns on media keys
  • Simple mode — floating title & artist, no background
  • Lives in your menu bar + Dock

03NEEDLEBOX

Your library,
in tune.

Essentia key & BPMCamelot wheelSet builder

Needlebox reads the key, BPM and energy of every track with Essentia, auto-sorts your library into harmonic folders, and lays out set running orders along a build → peak → cool arc. Search by vibe, mix on the Camelot wheel, export to .m3u8.

  • Accurate key + BPM (Essentia)
  • Camelot wheel + harmonic matches
  • Set Builder with an energy curve
  • AI tags, insights & vibe search
