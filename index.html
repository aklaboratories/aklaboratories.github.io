PRISM

Knows the key.
Every time.

Live keyCamelot wheelHarmonic mixing

Prism captures your system audio and detects the musical key live — chroma analysis run straight onto the Camelot wheel. DJ mode lays out your harmonic mixes; Producer mode hands back the chords, scales and progressions that actually fit.

  • Live key & Camelot read-out
  • DJ + Producer modes
  • Harmonic "mix next" suggestions
  • Optional AI directions

HALO

Your music,
wearing glass.

Any appAlbum-colour meshCompact mode

Halo floats a see-through widget of whatever's playing — Spotify, Apple Music, a browser tab, a podcast. The backdrop is a living mesh gradient pulled straight from the album art. Hit minimize and it shrinks to just the cover.

  • Universal now-playing
  • Mesh backdrop from album colours
  • Compact cover-only mode

About the creator

Made by one person
who cares about sound.

I'm Arjun Kalbag, a Computer Engineering and Economics student from New York City at Washington University in St. Louis with a strong interest in technology, design, and creative tools. Through AK Labs, I design and develop native macOS applications, primarily focused on music and the people who make it. Projects like Prism and Halo began as solutions to problems I encountered myself and evolved through iterative development into polished products. My experience spans software engineering, product development, and user-centered design, allowing me to build tools that combine technical functionality with intuitive user experiences.
